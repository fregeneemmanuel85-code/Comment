Our Story

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Lagos, ChopLife started as a small buka (local eatery) with a big dream: to share authentic Nigerian flavors with the world.

Every dish we serve carries the warmth of home cooking. From our smoky party jollof cooked over firewood-flavored stoves to our freshly grilled suya marinated in secret northern spices, we bring Nigeria's rich culinary heritage to your table.
